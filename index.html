Features

Play Me gives you awesome and unique features below

  • Music like Radio

    Just hit 'Play' and let app do the rest. It automatically finds and wakes up the melody you need.

  • Endless Playback

    Just tell the app what genres do you want to play. Play Me does the selection for you in an endless way.

  • Favorite Track

    Just mark tracks as favorite, and you can listen to them again anytime.

  • Gorgeous Design

    Design with a gorgeous in mind. Smooth and unique animation make your melody flies.

    App prepares for you with the most popular genres. But you can easily add your own wanted genres.

    Turn on the music, and let it slowly get down while you are sleeping.
